Museum of You

你的音乐博物馆

Listening to music is the most frequent emotional act we have, yet "my music" is forever just a cold list. So I built this: an AI reads the songs you've played this past year, reads your taste, and curates them into a 3D private museum you walk through in first person — your most-played track becomes the spotlit centerpiece, every exhibit gets a one-line caption, and swapping in a different playlist re-curates the whole place into a new mood, across six styles. Built for a Tencent Music AI hackathon; best on desktop.
